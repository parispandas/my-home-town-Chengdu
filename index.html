Chengdu,
The Gentle Capital of Sichuan

A city of relaxation, great food, and a refined art of living, Chengdu captivates with its laid-back rhythm and warm atmosphere. Blending modern districts, ancient traditions, and bold flavours, it offers a unique lifestyle that visitors quickly fall in love with.

⭐ Top 3 Things to Experience in Chengdu

Chengdu Research Base of Giant Pandas

The absolute must-see. Observe giant pandas in a protected environment — from playful adults to adorable cubs, and even red pandas. Tip: Visit in the morning when the pandas are most active.

Sichuan
Super hotpot

Chengdu’s signature dining experience. A fragrant, spicy broth infused with Sichuan peppercorn where you cook meat, vegetables, tofu, and noodles. Expect the famous “mala” flavour — spicy with a pleasantly numbing sensation.

Jinli Ancient Street & Wuhou Temple

Stroll through a traditional street filled with lanterns, local snacks, and handcrafted souvenirs. Right next door, explore Wuhou Temple, dedicated to Zhuge Liang and the Three Kingdoms heritage. A great spot to soak in culture, atmosphere, and take beautiful photos.
